About us

Kevin Escourse

Owner

As a consultant, Kevin has accrued over 10 years of vast experience in helping home owners, management agencies, developers, licensed home-improvement contractors, and registered design professionals to fulfill the requirements of securing project approval, build out and completion. The vibrant passion that Kevin has illustrated is heavily attributed to his inheritance of skilled-workmanship from his lineage of two generations of builders from his family.

Jahmal Sabaha

Registered Professional Engineer

With a bachelor's degree from Brown University and a multitude of experience and knowledge, Jahmal specializes in Structural Engineering and provides unparalleled support and consultation services on every project that the company acquires.

Marvin Escourse

Vice President

With an in depth understanding of the NYC Building Codes and Zoning regulations, Marvin oversees the day to day operations of the company. Combining his vast field experience and knowledge, Marvin works in a steadfast and professional manner and ensures that each project is completed to the highest standards.

Govinda Rampersaud

Manager

More commonly known as Buddy, he is responsible for managing all existing and potential projects of the company. With an in-depth knowledge of the intricacies of the Department of Buildings and their inner workings, Buddy has made seemingly insurmountable tasks possible to do.
